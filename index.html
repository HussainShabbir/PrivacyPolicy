Privacy Policy for Hit the Hue

Last updated: 08-October-2024

At Hit the Hue, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of Hit the Hue. The app does not require any personal data to function.

Advertising

Our app displays ads provided by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Interactions with ads (e.g., clicks or impressions)

This information helps the ad networks provide relevant advertisements to you.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display ads in our app. For more information about how Google AdMob collects and uses your information, please review their Privacy Policy at Google Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Hit the Hue does not collect or share any personal or sensitive user data with any parties. However, third-party ad networks (like Google AdMob) may collect and share non-personal data as described in their policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since Hit the Hue does not collect personal information, we do not retain any user data. All interactions with ads are handled by third-party networks in accordance with their privacy policies.
